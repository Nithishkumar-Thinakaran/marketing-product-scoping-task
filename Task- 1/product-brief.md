## Product Brief
------------
## Product Name : Marketing Performance Insight Dashboard

--------------------------------
### Problem Statement

Marketing teams often struggle to track campaign performance across multiple channels because data is scattered across different platforms. Reporting is mostly manual, time-consuming, and inconsistent.

-------------------------------------------
### Proposed Solution

Develop an internal dashboard that combines marketing data into a single platform. The dashboard will help teams monitor campaign performance, compare channels, and quickly identify where to focus marketing efforts.

-----------------------------------------------------
### Target Users
* Marketing analysts
* Marketing managers
* Internal leadership teams

------------------------------
### Core Features (V1)
* Unified marketing dashboard
* KPI tracking (CTR, conversions, ROI, spend)
* Date range filtering
* Channel performance comparison
* Basic performance insights

-------------------------------------
### Data Sources
* Google Ads
* Meta Ads
* Email marketing platforms
* Analytics tools

----------------
### Expected Benefits
* Faster reporting
* Reduced manual work
* Better decision-making
* Centralized marketing visibility
